**Osteoarthritis (OA)** is the most common form of arthritis, affecting millions worldwide. It occurs when the protective cartilage that cushions the ends of bones wears down over time. While it can damage any joint, OA most commonly affects joints in the hands, knees, hips, and spine.

• Most common in adults over 50
• Symptoms include pain, stiffness, and swelling
